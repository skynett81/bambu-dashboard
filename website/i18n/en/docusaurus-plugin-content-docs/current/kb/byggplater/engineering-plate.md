# Engineering Plate (Textured PEI)

The Engineering Plate is a textured PEI plate designed for technical materials such as PETG, ABS, ASA, and PA. It gives a fine textured bottom surface and is the most versatile plate in the Bambu Lab lineup.

## Properties

- **Surface:** Textured PEI — gives a patterned/matte bottom surface on prints
- **Adhesion:** Better mechanical grip than smooth PEI
- **Max bed temperature:** 110 °C
- **Release:** Prints release easily after cooling to 40–50 °C

## Best suited for

- PETG and PETG-CF (without glue stick, or with for extra safety)
- ABS and ASA (with glue stick)
- PA6 / PA12 / PA-CF (with glue stick — required)
- TPU (without glue stick)
- PLA (works well, gives textured bottom surface)

## Not suited for

- Prints requiring a smooth bottom surface (use Cool Plate)
- PC at high temperatures (use High Temp Plate)

## Maintenance

### Cleaning
```bash
# Weekly (or after glue stick use):
1. Wash with warm water and mild dish soap
2. Rinse thoroughly
3. Let dry, or dry with lint-free paper
4. Final wipe with IPA

# Between prints without glue stick:
1. Wipe with IPA and lint-free paper
```

### After glue stick
Always wash the plate after using glue stick. Remaining glue stick residue can cause uneven adhesion on the next print.

:::tip Texture direction
The Engineering Plate has a direction to its texture. Always mount the plate with clips in the correct orientation to avoid prints getting stuck in the texture indentations.
:::

## Replacing the plate

The Engineering Plate is worn when:
- The texture is worn smooth/flat in the center zone (most used area)
- Prints don't adhere even after cleaning and glue stick
- The plate has deep scratches or deformations

Normal lifespan: 300–700 prints with good maintenance.
